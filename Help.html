Filling out the survey?

To fill out the survey follow these steps:
  1. Click "Fill Out Survey" on the main page.
  2. Fill in the personal details, note any incomplete field will result in the survey NOT being submitted.
  3. Slect your favour food
  4. Select a rating for each of the 4 question.
  5. Click Submit, if anything is missing the survey will require re-entry else your survey will be submitted.

Viewing Results

To view your survey results follow these steps:
  1. Click "View Survey Results" on the main page.
  2. The results will be displayed for all people who complete the survey.
If you are still on the Survey page click "Back To Main" on the top left of you screen first then follow the above steps.
